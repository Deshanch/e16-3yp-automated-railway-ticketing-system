Automated Railway Ticketing System

Better Future.

Get Started

Problem

In srilanka people waste there valuable time by spending in long queues in railway stations. Eventhough people donot get better experiance when travelling. This problem effects badly on every aspects in day to day life of passengers.

And another thing is using cardboard tickets for entrances increases environmental pollusions and keeping several officers for ticket issueing increase the government expenses.

Solution

Introducing fully automated system which consisted of automatic gates and realtime web services.

This reduces the time wastage as passengers have several entrances and no unefficient human involvements for ticketing. No environmental pollusion any more as no tickets in the system. This will make the life easier.

Watch Introduction video

Solution Architecture

Every passenger should enroll to the department and when he/she enroll will get a plastic card which has a key to his/her account. Passenger can recharge their account at the cashier. When he/she enters to the platform he/she only have to swipe the card and the starting point will be added to the database.

Then they can seated anywhere he/she like Ticket checker will swipe their card again and class(1,2,3) will be added to the database. Finally passenger should swipe their card at the destination station. Then the cost will be deducted from their account. The gate at the destination will be closed until this procedure for each passenger.

All money transactions and travelling history can be tracked and a database can be managed. All of the details related to passengers account can be managed by using app or website.

System Flow

Initially passenger have to register in our system and then he/she will be given a passenger card and then he/she can swipe the card and enter the station. At the entrance our system will verify the passenger account, check that there is sufficient money in the passsenger account(the minimum balance should be 500.00 RS) or any other system error like account freezing.Account freezing will happen when the passenger leave destination station without paying the travel cost.

If there is no such error and if there is no any illegal activity at his entrance, then he can enter to the train.When entering to the train his/her account will be holded until he/she leaves destination after swiping.In the train ticket checker checks about the passenger class.Then at the exit, passenger has to swipe the card and system releases the holded accuont and if the passenger don't swipe the card then account will be freezed. In the freeze state what happens is, he can't enter from an entrance gate without paying our fine.

After that using the travel details, system automatically do the money balances and all the updated details will be sent to the database.
